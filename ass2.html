EcoSea

    Objective Description Features Why Chosen

Objectives

  • Promote sustainable seafood sourcing and reduce overfishing.
  • Provide an online marketplace connecting certified sustainable fishers with environmentally conscious consumers.
  • Offer transparency on seafood sourcing, making it easier for consumers to make eco-friendly choices.
  • Educate users on the impact of sustainable seafood consumption on marine ecosystems.

    • Description

    Educate users on the impact of sustainable seafood consumption on marine ecosystems.

    Features

    1. Seafood Listings: A searchable and filterable list of certified sustainable seafood options. Each listing includes detailed information about the fishing method, origin, and certification status.
    2. Eco-Certification Check: Ensures sellers meet sustainable standards before listing, reinforcing buyer confidence in seafood origins.
    3. Price and Source Transparency: Each seafood listing includes transparent pricing, a breakdown of costs, and detailed sourcing information, emphasizing transparency in the supply chain.
    4. Educational Content: A section offering articles, videos, and infographics on sustainable fishing, the impact of overfishing, and ways to support marine conservation.
    5. Eco-Badge System for Sellers: Fishers earn badges for meeting various sustainability milestones, motivating adherence to sustainable practices.
    6. Consumer Rating and Feedback: Allows users to rate and review products, contributing to a community that values sustainable seafood.
    7. Subscription Discounts: Regular consumers can subscribe to monthly seafood boxes, receiving discounts and exclusive deals, supporting regular sustainable purchases.

    Why Chosen

    With growing awareness of the ecological damage caused by overfishing, EcoSea aims to help mitigate this issue by promoting eco-friendly seafood options. By supporting sustainable fisheries, consumers contribute to the health of marine ecosystems, helping maintain biodiversity and food security for future generations.
