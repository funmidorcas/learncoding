Our Mission

EcoSea is committed to advancing Sustainable Development Goal 14, Life Below Water. By supporting sustainable seafood sourcing and reducing overfishing, we aim to preserve marine biodiversity and promote eco-friendly consumer choices. Join us in building a healthier relationship with our oceans.

"EcoSea: Sustainable Fisheries Marketplace" is an online platform dedicated to promoting eco-friendly seafood sourcing by connecting certified sustainable fishers with conscious consumers.
